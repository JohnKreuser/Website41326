John J. Kreuser, EI

Engineering Designer II – Electrical

Personal Summary

John J. Kreuser is an Engineering Designer II, EI located in Pensacola, Florida. His experience includes low voltage power distribution, lighting, fibre optic distribution, telecommunications, grounding and lightning protection systems, industrial instrumentation and control systems, SCADA networks, and fire protection systems. He has power system study experience including overcurrent protective device coordination, short-circuit analysis, load flows, and arc flash hazard analysis. This technical experience has been in the Water / Wastewater sector across the United States. His software experience includes, but is not limited to, Power Tools for Windows by SKM Systems Analysis, Inc., AGI32 by Lighting Analysts, and various versions of Bentley MicroStation and Autodesk AutoCAD and Revit. His responsibilities include client relations, engineering design, specification development, submittal review, and construction management.
